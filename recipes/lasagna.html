Mommas Lasagna, from the Old Country

The finished meal

A tasty italian meal, with laywers of pasta and sauce. A real taste of the old country, straight from the oven and directly into your heart.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Red Sauce
  • Filling
  • White Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make red sauce.
  2. Layer pasta and red sauce.
  3. Top with white sauce and then cheese.
  4. Enjoy the love.
