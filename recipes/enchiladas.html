Mexican dish that's bursting with flavour.

Here's one we made earlier.

One of our favourites. Chicken, cheese, peppers, tomatoes, onions and spices make this a really filling treat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Spices
  • Cheese
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Fry the chicken.
  2. Add spices and vegtables.
  3. Wrap mixture in tortillas.
  4. Top with cheese.
  5. Bake for twenty minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
